Methodology

How We Build

01
Design Validation & BIM
Before a single brick is laid, we validate structural, architectural, and MEP designs in 3D using Building Information Modelling — eliminating conflicts before they cost time or money.
02
Foundation & Piling
Bengaluru's varied geology demands a scientific approach — soil testing, pile load tests, and raft design calibrated to the specific site, not copied from a generic template.
03
Superstructure
RCC frame construction with high-grade cement and TMT steel, poured to IS 456 standards and tested via cube testing every slab cycle.
04
Façade & Envelope
Brickwork, glazing, external finishes, and waterproofing applied with a 15-year warranty — because a building's skin is as important as its skeleton.
05
MEP Coordination
All mechanical, electrical, and plumbing works executed by our in-house certified teams — fully tested and BESCOM/BWSSB approved before handover.
06
Snag-Free Handover
A 240-point snagging checklist and independent QC audit ensures every unit is handed over truly defect-free — not just 'functionally complete'.
